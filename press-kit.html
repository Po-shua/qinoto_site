PRESS KIT

Qinoto Qinoto is a Paper-folding puzzle game, inspired by MAD Fold-Ins,  that utilises multi-touch to deliver a unique gameplay experience, where you can  fold the game world like paper to revealing new objects & patterns hidden in the scenes.

KEY FEATURES:
- A new way of utilising multi-touch 
- Unique folding gameplay mechanic
- New secrets are revealed as you fold 
- A unique blend of traditional puzzles, riddles and optical illusions.
